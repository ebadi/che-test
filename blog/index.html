Chedoku Puzzle

To solve a Chedoku puzzle, simply arrange the chess pieces so that the number of threatening pieces matches the large numbers provided. The large numbers show how many pieces threaten each square in the solution. i.e. move the pieces around to match the large and the small numbers. Play it for free now.
